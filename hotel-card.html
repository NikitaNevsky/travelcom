About the hotel
The four—star Holiday Inn Paris - Bastille is located in the center of Paris, 200 meters from Gare de Lyon and a two-minute walk from Gare de Lyon Metro Station. The Place de la Bastille is just 700 metres from the hotel.
The Holiday Inn Paris — Bastille belongs to the Holiday Inn Hotels & Resorts hotel chain and offers its guests to enjoy a comfortable stay.
A free buffet breakfast is served daily in the hotel's restaurant. Upon request, breakfast can be served in the room. Guests can enjoy a variety of drinks at the lounge bar. Free Wi‑Fi is available throughout the hotel.
The Holiday Inn Paris — Bastille has 128 stylish rooms. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, an LCD TV with satellite channels and a minibar. Each room also has a private bathroom with free toiletries.
